About DataLearn10X

We help learners become job-ready data professionals through practical, project-based programs and focused mentorship.

Who We Are

DataLearn10X is an outcome-driven learning platform focused on Data Analytics and related tools. Our programs are designed for beginners as well as professionals who want to switch careers, upskill, and build confidence with real-world workflows.

🎯 Our Mission

To make analytics education simple, affordable, and practical so learners can confidently apply skills in real jobs.

🧩 What Makes Us Different

Structured roadmaps, practical assignments, interview-oriented preparation, and mentor support at every stage.

🛠 Tools You Learn

Advance Excel, Python, Power BI, Tableau, SQL (MySQL), and Machine Learning + Deep Learning fundamentals.

📌 Learner Outcomes

Portfolio projects, dashboard building confidence, improved problem-solving, and readiness for analytics interviews.

How We Support You

  • Step-by-step learning paths from beginner to job-ready level.
  • Real examples and project tasks aligned with business scenarios.
  • Mentor guidance and doubt support to keep you on track.
  • Interview-focused preparation for roles in data analytics.
